## Philosophy

I build software guided by a clear philosophy: identify the right problems, solve them effectively, and **avoid unnecessary complexity**. My minimalist approach ensures I deliver exactly what's needed, then continuously refine and enhance it. I rely on proven, stable technologies—tools that offer predictability, reliability, and efficiency. 

While innovation is valuable, I prioritize **trustworthiness and durability** in my solutions. Simple, clear code isn't just better; it's essential. I emphasize **readability, maintainability, and reliability** above all else. 

Principles like KISS (Keep It Simple, Stupid) and DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) are fundamental to my practice, forming the bedrock of scalable and maintainable systems. If it isn't clear, concise, and trustworthy, the work isn't finished.
